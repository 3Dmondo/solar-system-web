Processing notes:
NASA 3D Resources JPG files were adopted directly. The Titan source JPG was downloaded from NASA Photojournal at 5760x2880, resized locally to 2048x1024 JPG quality 88 with System.Drawing high-quality bicubic interpolation, and the large source original was not kept in git.
Titan conversion command summary: resize source JPG to 2048x1024 with System.Drawing HighQualityBicubic interpolation and save JPEG quality 88 to assets/textures/2k_titan.jpg.
