# Star Catalog Data Attribution

## Star Catalog (catalog.json)

**Source:** HYG Database v4.2
**URL:** https://codeberg.org/astronexus/hyg
**License:** Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0)
**Processing:** Filtered to apparent magnitude ≤ 6.5 (naked-eye visible stars)

The HYG database combines data from:
- Hipparcos Catalogue (ESA)
- Yale Bright Star Catalog (5th Edition)
- Gliese Catalog of Nearby Stars (3rd Edition)

## Constellation Lines (constellations.json)

**Source:** IAU constellation patterns via d3-celestial
**URL:** https://github.com/ofrohn/d3-celestial
**License:** BSD-3-Clause
**Processing:** Converted to RA (hours) / Dec (degrees) format
